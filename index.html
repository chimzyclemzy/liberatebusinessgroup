Preparing your Business for Sale

At Liberate Business Group, our goal is to ensure your business is fit and ready to be taken to the market. This will optimise your sale outcomes, whether that be selling all or a part of your business.

We are experts in mentoring businesses and going through a process to set them up for sale.

We will spend time with you understanding the key drivers of you and your business and how your business operates. We will then work with you to custom design a strategy unique to your business.  

LEARN MORE!

Why? | Benefits

Maximum Sale Price

If your business is tidy and well documented it is easier to sell. It is easier for someone to undertake due diligence and clears up any questions about how you do things.

Faster Sale


A business that is ready to go on the market will attract more interest. This is because you have gone through the process of ensuring that when someone comes in they will have access to everything they need.

Unique Value Proposition

By working through a process to get your business ready for sale you will understand your unique value proposition and why you started the business in the first place.

Peace of Mind


By working through a process to get the business ready it will give you peace of mind that you are presenting your business in the best possible light when you come to sell it.
